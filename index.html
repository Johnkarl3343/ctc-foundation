About the school

History

Ceguera Technological Colleges (CTC) in Iriga City, Philippines, was founded in 1984 by Julio D. Ceguera as the Ceguera Institute of Science and Technology (CIST) to provide affordable technical-vocational education. Renamed in 1998 to reflect its expansion into tertiary education, it remains a key provider of technical, vocational, and business programs in the Rinconada/Partido area

Historical Development

  • 1984: Founding. The school was originally registered as the Ceguera Institute of Science and Technology (CIST) on January 19, 1984. It initially focused on vocational programs such as Diesel Mechanic Technology, Machine Shop Technology, and Dressmaking.
  • 1986–1987: Expansion to Tertiary Education. The institution began offering degree programs, including a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Education (BSIE) and a Bachelor of Arts (AB) in Mathematics.
  • 1998: Name Change. The school was renamed Ceguera Technological Colleges (CTC) to reflect its transition into a tertiary education provider. This change was officially registered with the SEC on October 30, 1998.
  • 2000s: Program Diversification. CTC expanded its curriculum to include BS in Computer Science (2002), BS in Criminology (2003), and BS in Office Administration (2007).
  • 2016: Senior High School. The college launched its first batch of Senior High School students under the Department of Education’s K-12 program.2016: Senior High School. The college launched its first batch of Senior High School students under the Department of Education’s K-12 program.
  • 2024–2026: Recent Milestones. The college celebrated its 41st Anniversary in 2025 and its 42nd Anniversary in February 2026 under the theme "Where Vision Endures"

Message from Our President

As we celebrate the 42nd Foundation Anniversary of Ceguera Technological Colleges, we proudly reflect on forty-two years of dedication, growth, and excellence in education. Together, we honor our past achievements while confidently moving forward to shape a brighter future for the next generation. Happy 42nd Foundation Anniversary to the entire CTC community!
